**Researcher in Tribology, Mechanochemistry, and Scientific Machine Learning**  
Institute of Machine Tools and Production Technology (IWF)  
Leibniz University Hannover

I study how surfaces interact, wear, and transform under extreme conditions.  
My work combines **tribology**, **mechanochemistry**, **surface engineering**, and **data‑driven modeling** to understand and design materials for demanding environments.

- 🔬 *Mechanochemical activation & wear mechanisms*  
- ⚙️ *Tribological coatings under extreme environments*  
- 🧪 *Surface topography & transfer film formation*  
- 🤖 *Scientific machine learning for materials design*
